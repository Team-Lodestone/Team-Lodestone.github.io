Project Lodestone

Edit, Convert and Transform Minecraft Worlds.

The first open-source, all-in-one Minecraft tool, compatible with nearly all platforms,
versions, and editions, created by the community for the community.

 Join us on Discord

Compatible

It works both as an app and as a web-app, allowing you to access it from anywhere, on any device.

Fun

Extensive customization options paired with user-friendly design ensure an enjoyable experience.

Agile

Optimized for speed and multi-threading, it minimizes resources and prevents memory leaks.

Extendable

Extensions, plugins, and scripts are supported, unlocking endless possibilities.

Convenient

Built-in resigners grant PS3/Vita world access and region conversion without third-party tools!

Accessible

Several settings and optimizations for visual disabilities ensure accessibility for all.

Smart

The toolset evolves with Minecraft updates, keeping all users up-to-date effortlessly.

Adaptive

Supporting all major languages, it thrives on open-source community contributions.

Helpful

Debug like a pro with built-in tools, with more updates on the way.
